Problem Statement:


The wine industry faces the challenge of subjective quality assessments, significantly affecting consumer satisfaction and ROI. Traditional tasting methods show high variability among experts, underscoring the need for a more objective, consistent approach. Research suggests that machine learning can effectively predict wine quality by analyzing physicochemical properties, thereby addressing the critical issue of inconsistency in wine ratings, especially those below 5.
