Step 11 - Waterfall Chart





To modify the waterfall chart for wine quality attributes follow these steps:

  1. Select "Waterfall" as the chart type within your visualization options.
  2. Drag the "Measure %" from the "Waterfall %" table into the "Category" area of the chart. Afterward, pull the " Chemical Rate " measure into the "Values" area. Be sure to set the "Chemical Rate" measure to reflect percentage values.
  3. Measure % calculates changes only when there's a non-zero difference between the new and original values displaying increase and decrease percentages (Green & Red), thus tracking variations in wine properties across different periods effectively. 
  • Set the waterfall chart's background color to #F7F3F3 with a transparency of 15%, distinguishing it from the other visuals, which maintain the same background color but with a lower transparency of 5%. This ensures that the background image is visible through the waterfall chart.
