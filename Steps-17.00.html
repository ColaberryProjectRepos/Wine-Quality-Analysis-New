Step 9 - First Chart:





To create a wine quality visual in Power BI using the Line and Stacked Column Chart:

  1. From the Visualizations pane, select the 'Line and Stacked Column Chart' and place it in the designated area of your Power BI report.
  2. Assign 'Quality' to the X-axis to categorize data. For the column values on the Y-axis, use 'Total Wines' to represent the quantity of each quality rating.
  3. Add line values on the Y-axis for 'Citric', 'Volatile', 'Sulphates', and 'Chlorides' to illustrate the percentage of each chemical component present at different quality levels.
  4. To clearly convey the data, name your chart 'Total Wines by Quality' and adjust the visual's title accordingly.
  5. For the color scheme, select unique and consistent colors for each chemical component line, ensuring they are distinct and avoid confusion with other dashboard elements.
  6. Sync the color coding with other related visuals across the dashboard to maintain a cohesive and intuitive user experience.
