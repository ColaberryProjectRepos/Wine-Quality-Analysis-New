Step 7 - KPI Cards:


Select a "Card" visualization and place it in the first box in our Top Left . This will be the "Total Wines" KPI measure . (The GIF at the bottom will give you a brief showcase of the formatting you need for this card) 
 
  • This KPI measure is a Total of Wine tested 
  • Total Wines = SUM('Wine-Quality'[Volume Tested (Liters)]) 


                              



  • This is the calculated column that was added in STEP 2
  • Volume Tested (Liters) = RAND() * (1000 - 500) + 500 


                             



For formatting KPI cards in Power BI:

  • Navigate to Visual > Background and set the color to #F7F3F3 with a transparency of 5%.
  • For titles, use DIN font at size 14 and set the text color to #12239E.
  • Maintain the same format for all KPIs except for the Callout Values color difference.





 Follow the same formatting as above GFI for the rest of KPIs.
